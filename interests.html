My Interests

Academic Interests

Growing up, I never really enjoyed maths or science; these subjects seemed daunting and uninteresting to me. However, as I matured, I discovered a newfound passion for them. What once felt like overwhelming challenges turned into exciting puzzles waiting to be solved. Through perseverance and dedication, I honed my skills and realized that I am talented in these areas.

Games and Sports

My interests are quite diverse, ranging from strategic games to action-packed adventures and athletic pursuits. I am an avid chess player, enjoying the mental challenge and strategic depth that the game offers. In the digital realm, I find excitement in playing Brawl Stars, PUBG, and Minecraft, each offering unique experiences and endless possibilities for creativity and competition. Beyond gaming, I have a strong passion for soccer, both playing and watching the sport. The teamwork, skill, and exhilaration of the game never fail to captivate me.

My Favourite Anime

I have a deep passion for watching anime, and some of my all-time favorites include Attack on Titan (AOT), Hunter x Hunter (HXH), Death Note, Tokyo Ghoul, and Dr. Stone. Each of these series has captivated me with its unique storytelling, intricate characters, and stunning visuals. Attack on Titan drew me in with its intense action and complex narrative, while Hunter x Hunter's adventurous spirit and clever plot twists kept me hooked. Death Note's psychological cat-and-mouse game and Tokyo Ghoul's dark, emotional journey left a lasting impact on me. Lastly, Dr. Stone's blend of science and survival is both educational and exhilarating.

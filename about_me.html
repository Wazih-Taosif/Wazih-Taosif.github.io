More About Me!

My Background

I was born in a South Asian Country called Bangladesh. And I lived there almost my entire childhood. I then moved to USA in order to persue my Secondary and Higher Education here. This significant move was fueled by my determination to explore new horizons, gain advanced expertise, and achieve academic excellence. However, despite my physical distance from Bangladesh, my heart remains deeply connected to my roots. I embody a strong sense of patriotism and remain actively engaged with the Bangladeshi community.

My Education

I completed my primary, middle, as well as my 9th and 10th grade education from 'Chittagong Grammar School National Curriculum.' Which is one of Bangladesh's most esteemed educational institutions. It's renowned for its acedemic excellence and holistic approach to education. This school provided an environment that nurtured my intellectual curiosity and personal growth.
CGS NC

My educational journey took a significant turn when I attended High School For Global Citizenship in New York City. HSGC is celebrated for its high academic standards and innovative educational approach, offering students a unique and enriching learning environment. The school's dedicated faculty and cutting-edge facilities provided the perfect backdrop for my academic and personal growth.

It offers a unique learning environment where students engage in hands-on, project-based education. In here I focused on learning Computer Science, but primarily Full Stack Development.
